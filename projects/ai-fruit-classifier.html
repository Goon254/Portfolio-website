Convolutional Neural Network Architecture

The model employs a sophisticated CNN architecture designed specifically for fruit classification. The network consists of multiple convolutional layers with ReLU activation, batch normalization, and dropout for regularization, followed by fully connected layers for final classification.

model = Sequential([ Conv2D(32, (3,3), activation='relu', input_shape=(224,224,3)), BatchNormalization(), MaxPooling2D(2,2), Conv2D(64, (3,3), activation='relu'), BatchNormalization(), MaxPooling2D(2,2), Conv2D(128, (3,3), activation='relu'), BatchNormalization(), MaxPooling2D(2,2), Flatten(), Dense(512, activation='relu'), Dropout(0.5), Dense(num_classes, activation='softmax') ])
